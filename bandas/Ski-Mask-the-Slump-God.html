Stokeley Clevon Goulbourne (born April 18, 1996), known professionally as Ski Mask the Slump God (formerly stylized as $ki Mask "The Slump God"), is an American rapper and songwriter. He originally collaborated with fellow American rapper XXXTentacion and their collective Members Only, and in 2017 released his songs "Catch Me Outside" and "Babywipe", which are both featured on his mixtape YouWillRegret and certified Gold by the RIAA.
