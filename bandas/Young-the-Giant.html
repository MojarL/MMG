Young the Giant es una banda de indie rock estadounidense formada en Irvine, California, en 2004. La banda está formada por Sameer Gadhia (voz principal), Jacob Tilley (guitarra), Eric Cannata (guitarra), Payam Doostzadeh (bajo), y François Comtois (batería). Anteriormente conocido como The Jakes, Young the Giant fue firmado por Roadrunner Records en 2009 y lanzó su álbum debut homónimo en 2010. La banda de los dos primeros sencillos, "My Body" y "Cough Syrup", llegó a los cinco primeros de la tabla de EE.UU. Alternative Songs.


Young the Giant is an American rock band that formed in Irvine, California, in 2004. The band's line-up consists of Sameer Gadhia (lead vocals), Jacob Tilley (guitar), Eric Cannata (guitar), Payam Doostzadeh (bass guitar), and Francois Comtois (drums). Formerly known as The Jakes, Young the Giant was signed by Roadrunner Records in 2009, and they released their eponymous debut album in 2010. The band's first three singles, "My Body", "Cough Syrup", and "Apartment", all charted on the US Alternative Songs chart.
