NCT (en hangul, 엔시티; romanización revisada del coreano, Enssiti) es una boy band multinacional formada por S.M. Entertainment en 2016. Tiene 21 miembros, Su nombre proviene de Neo Culture Technology, un término acuñado por el fundador de S.M., Lee Soo-man, describió el concepto del grupo de tener un número ilimitado de miembros divididos en múltiples subunidades basadas en varias ciudades en todo el mundo.


NCT (Korean: 엔시티) is a South Korean boy group formed by SM Entertainment. Their name stands for the Hallyu localization project Neo Culture Technology, a term coined by their producer and SM founder Lee Soo-man to describe the group's concept of having an unlimited number of members divided into multiple sub-units based in various cities worldwide. Since the group's initial introduction by their native label's founder in January 2016, the group consists of 21 members as of January 2019, aging from their mid-teens to mid-twenties and participating in a total of four different sub-units.
